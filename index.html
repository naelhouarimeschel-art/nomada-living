Our Story

About Nomada Living

Nomada Living was founded with a singular vision: to bring European standards of outdoor living excellence to the UAE and GCC market. We partner with the most respected manufacturers in Spain and the Netherlands to deliver products simply unavailable elsewhere in the region.

With over 800 completed residential installations across Europe and a growing portfolio of landmark hospitality and commercial projects in the UAE, we combine international expertise with deep local knowledge of the region's climate, regulations and aesthetic expectations.

Our turnkey approach means a single point of contact from concept to completion — ensuring seamless delivery on even the most complex outdoor projects.
